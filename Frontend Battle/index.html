About MITS

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1957

Madhav Institute of Technology & Science (MITS), Gwalior — established in 1957 under the aegis of the Scindia Education Society — has been declared an Institution Deemed to be University under Distinct Category by the Ministry of Education, Government of India, on the advice of UGC (March 2024). Accredited with the prestigious NAAC A++ Grade, MITS stands as a beacon of engineering excellence in Central India.

  • To provide quality education in technical and allied disciplines
  • To organize innovative courses in Engineering and Technology
  • To arrange vocational courses in upcoming fields to meet global advancement
  • To promote research in Technology and Science

Dr. R.K. Pandit

Vice Chancellor, MITS-DU
